## Goals and Requirments
- standard 250mm x 250mm
- CoreXY
- Enclosed
- at least 150mm/s
- Klipper
- Under $250 (is under $200 worth the pain?)
- Open-Source
- won't burn my house down (I hope)
- Rigid for good printing qualtiy (Shut up I spelled that right. YOU ARE THE WRONG ONE)
